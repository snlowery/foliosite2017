Design

I was able to validate many of my feature ideas and interaction designs with user testing. At each new stage of iteration on the design I would test. With the help of my colleague in marketing I spearheaded the creation of a user group. This group allowed us to recruit participants very quickly.



Launch

We launched with a simple un-themed version of the product for adults and a gamified version for kids. Users were able to track their reading, complete challenges, win awards, and work through a story as they leveled up through the game.
